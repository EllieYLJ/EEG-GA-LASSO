<h3 data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="margin-top: 30px; margin-bottom: 15px; padding: 0px; font-weight: bold; color: black; font-size: 20px;"><span class="prefix" style="display: none;"></span><span class="content">About EDRA</span><span class="suffix" style="display: none;"></span></h3>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="font-size: 16px; padding-top: 8px; padding-bottom: 8px; margin: 0; line-height: 26px; color: black;">We present a publicly available dataset for analysis of the risk of depression among college students, called the EDRA dataset. The dataset contains 50 subjects, who were all students of Henan University. Moreover, they were classified into high score group (i.e., high-risk depression group) and low score group (i.e., low-risk control group). Before the EEG data recording, depression was assessed by the PROMIS (patient-reported outcomes measurement information system) depression scale (8a version). EEG data were collected by using 62 electrodes in an international standard EEG cap and a position above the right eye. The sampling frequency is 500 Hz.</p>
<h3 data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="margin-top: 30px; margin-bottom: 15px; padding: 0px; font-weight: bold; color: black; font-size: 20px;"><span class="prefix" style="display: none;"></span><span class="content">Dataset Ethics</span><span class="suffix" style="display: none;"></span></h3>
<p data-tool="mdnice编辑器" style="font-size: 16px; padding-top: 8px; padding-bottom: 8px; margin: 0; line-height: 26px; color: black;">These subjects obtained informed consent before the experiment. The experiments were conducted under the Declaration of Helsinki and approved by the Ethics Committee of Henan University (HUSOM-2018-367).</p>
